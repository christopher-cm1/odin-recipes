Tofu Teriyaki Bowl

Tofu Teriyaki bowl

Description

Tofu is a high protein vegan alternative that makes a great addition to a variety of diets,Grilling the veggies and tofu adds so much flavor to this vegetarian dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package extra firm tofu, drained and pressed
  • ⅔ cup teriyaki sauce, divided
  • 1 large red bell pepper
  • 2 small zucchini
  • ½ fresh pineapple, peeled and cored
  • 1 large onion
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 cups hot cooked rice
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Slice tofu into 4 thick slices. Marinate in 1/3 cup of teriyaki sauce while you prepare the vegetables.
  2. Cut bell pepper into 4 wedges; remove core and seeds. Cut ends off zucchini, then cut each zucchini in half lengthwise. Slice pineapple into 4 wedges. Cut onion into quarters. Place vegetables and pineapple onto a large platter. Brush with olive oil and season vegetables with salt and pepper.
  3. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil grate.
  4. Grill onions for 4 minutes, turning halfway through. Add bell pepper, zucchini, and pineapple to the grill and continue grilling,Remove to a platter as each item finishes cooking, and cut into bite-sized pieces when cool enough to handle.
  5. Remove tofu from the marinade. Lightly oil the grate and grill 2 to 3 minutes per side.
  6. Divide hot rice between 4 bowls. Top with grilled vegetables, pineapple, and tofu. Drizzle with remaining 1/3 cup teriyaki sauce. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve.
